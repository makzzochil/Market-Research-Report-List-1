<p><strong>The Superabsorbent Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adherent</li><li>Non-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The superabsorbent dressing market is divided into adherent and non-adherent types. Adherent dressings stick to the wound, providing secure coverage and preventing leakage, making them suitable for moderate to heavily exuding wounds. Non-adherent dressings, on the other hand, do not adhere to the wound bed, minimizing pain during dressing changes and ideal for sensitive or fragile skin. Both types effectively manage exudate and maintain a moist wound environment, promoting faster healing and reducing the risk of infection.</p></p>
