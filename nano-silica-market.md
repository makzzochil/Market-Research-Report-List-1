<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano silica market is categorized into two main types: P-type and S-type. P-type nano silica is primarily produced through precipitation methods and is known for its high purity and specific surface area, making it valuable for applications like rubber reinforcement and coatings. S-type nano silica, on the other hand, is synthesized using a sol-gel process, offering unique properties suitable for electronics and pharmaceutical sectors. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing material performance across various industries.</p></p>
