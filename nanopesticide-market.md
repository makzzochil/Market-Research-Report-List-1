<p><p>The nanopesticide market features various advanced formulations enhancing pesticide efficiency and sustainability. Nanoemulsions are stable mixtures of oil and water that improve pesticide delivery and absorption. Nanocapsules encapsulate active ingredients for controlled release, minimizing environmental impact. Nanogels offer a soft, gel-like matrix to hold and release pesticides gradually. Nanofibers act as carriers, providing increased surface area for better interaction with pests. Lastly, nanoparticles enhance the bioavailability and effectiveness of active ingredients, driving innovation in pest management solutions.</p></p>
