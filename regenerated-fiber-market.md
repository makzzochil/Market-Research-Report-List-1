<p><p>The regenerated fiber market encompasses various types including rayon, acetate, cellulose, and lyocell. Rayon is derived from natural cellulose, providing a silk-like feel, often used in clothing and home textiles. Acetate, made from cellulose acetate, is known for its lustrous appearance and is commonly used in linings and formal wear. Cellulose fibers are environmentally friendly options derived from plants. Lyocell, produced using a closed-loop process, is biodegradable and renowned for its strength and comfort, making it popular in eco-conscious fashion.</p></p>
