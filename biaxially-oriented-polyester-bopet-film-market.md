<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyester (BOPET) film is a high-performance, flexible packaging material made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) that has been stretched in both the machine and transverse directions. This orientation enhances its physical properties, making BOPET films strong, transparent, and resistant to moisture, chemicals, and UV light. These characteristics make BOPET a popular choice in packaging, electrical insulation, printing, and various other applications.</p><p>The BOPET film market is poised for significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for lightweight, durable packaging solutions in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, electronics, and consumer goods.
