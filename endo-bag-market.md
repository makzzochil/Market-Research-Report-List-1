<p><strong>The Endo Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detachable</li><li>Non-detachable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endo Bag market is primarily divided into two types: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable endo bags can be removed from the laparoscopic instruments post-surgery, allowing for easier specimen retrieval and management. In contrast, non-detachable endo bags remain attached during the procedure, simplifying usage but potentially complicating specimen extraction. Both types cater to various surgical needs, with their selection influenced by factors such as surgical technique, procedure complexity, and surgeon preference, ensuring optimal outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
