<p><strong>The Glass Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chopped Strand</li><li>Continuous Filament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass mat market comprises primarily two types: chopped strand and continuous filament. Chopped strand mats are made of randomly oriented glass fibers held together with a binder, providing flexibility and strength for applications in automotive, construction, and marine sectors. Continuous filament mats, on the other hand, utilize continuous glass fibers uniformly laid to enhance structural integrity and performance, making them suitable for high-strength applications like aerospace and industrial components. Both types serve essential roles in various composite material applications.</p></p>
