<p><ul><li>Ordered Structure</li><li>Disordered Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous alloys are categorized into two primary market types based on their structural organization: ordered structure and disordered structure. Ordered structure alloys exhibit a certain degree of atomic arrangement or periodicity, offering improved stability and mechanical properties. In contrast, disordered structure alloys lack a regular arrangement, resulting in unique characteristics such as high strength and excellent resistance to corrosion. Both types serve diverse applications across industries, including electronics, aerospace, and automotive, driven by their distinct properties and performance capabilities.</p></p>
