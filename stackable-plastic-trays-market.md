<p><p>The stackable plastic trays market consists of three main types: Polypropylene (PP), Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), and Polyethylene (PE). PP trays are durable, heat-resistant, and widely used in food packaging due to their ability to withstand high temperatures. PET trays are known for their clarity, strength, and recyclability, making them popular for retail and presentation purposes. PE trays are flexible, lightweight, and moisture-resistant, suitable for agricultural and industrial applications. Each material offers distinct advantages, catering to varying industry needs.</p></p>
