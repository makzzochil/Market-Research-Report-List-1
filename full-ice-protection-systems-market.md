<p><strong>The Full Ice Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>De-Icing Systems</li><li>Anti-Icing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Ice Protection Systems market encompasses two main types: de-icing systems and anti-icing systems. De-icing systems actively remove ice after it has formed on aircraft surfaces, employing mechanisms like heated surfaces or pneumatic devices. In contrast, anti-icing systems prevent ice formation by maintaining surfaces at temperatures above freezing, often using heated materials or chemicals. Both systems are critical for ensuring safety and performance in aviation, particularly in icy conditions, enhancing aircraft reliability and operational efficiency.</p></p>
