<p><p>Copper foil for HDI (High-Density Interconnect) circuits is categorized by thickness, with types typically being 9 µm, 12 µm, and 18 µm. Thinner foils, like 9 µm, enable finer circuitry and higher layer density, making them ideal for advanced applications in smartphones and high-performance electronics. The 12 µm variant strikes a balance between manufacturing ease and electrical performance. In contrast, 18 µm foil is suited for more robust applications where durability and heat dissipation are crucial, accommodating moderate density requirements.</p></p>
