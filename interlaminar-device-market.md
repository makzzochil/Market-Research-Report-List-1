<p><strong>The Interlaminar Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interspinous Distraction Devices</li><li>Coflex Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interlaminar device market primarily comprises interspinous distraction devices and the Coflex device. Interspinous distraction devices are designed to provide relief by creating space between adjacent spinous processes, alleviating pressure on spinal nerves. In contrast, the Coflex device functions as a flexible spacer that stabilizes the spine while maintaining the natural motion, making it suitable for treating spinal stenosis and other degenerative conditions. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes by offering less invasive options for spinal surgery.</p></p>
