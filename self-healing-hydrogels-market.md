<p><strong>The Self-healing Hydrogels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Crosslinking</li><li>Physical Crosslinking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-healing hydrogels market is primarily categorized into two types: chemical crosslinking and physical crosslinking. Chemical crosslinking involves covalent bonds that enhance stability and durability, allowing hydrogels to recover from damage through molecular rearrangement. In contrast, physical crosslinking relies on non-covalent interactions, such as hydrogen bonds or ionic interactions, which offer reversible bonding and flexibility. Both types cater to diverse applications, including biomedical, tissue engineering, and soft robotics, showcasing their importance in advanced material development.</p></p>
