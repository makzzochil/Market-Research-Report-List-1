<p><p>The purified water generation system market can be categorized into three main types: UV-based, RO-based, and gravity-based systems. UV-based systems utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate microorganisms, ensuring safe drinking water. RO-based systems employ reverse osmosis technology to remove impurities and sediments, producing highly purified water. Gravity-based systems rely on natural gravitational forces to filter and purify water, often using sedimentation and slow filtration methods. Each type serves distinct user needs, ranging from residential to industrial applications.</p></p>
