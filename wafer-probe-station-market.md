<p><p>The wafer probe station market is categorized into three types based on automation levels: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual probe stations require operators to manually position probes on wafers, offering flexibility but lower throughput. Semi-automatic systems enhance efficiency by automating some processes while still requiring operator involvement. Fully automatic probe stations operate independently, allowing for high-speed, precise testing with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production environments and advanced semiconductor testing applications.</p></p>
