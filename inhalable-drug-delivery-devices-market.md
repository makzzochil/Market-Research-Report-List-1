<p><p>Inhalable drug delivery devices are crucial for administering medications directly to the lungs. Pressurized Metered Dose Inhalers (PMDIs) deliver precise doses using propellant to aerosolize the drug. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist for easy inhalation, ideal for patients with difficulty using other devices. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) release medication in a powdered form, requiring patients to inhale deeply to activate the dose. Other devices may include soft mist inhalers and vibrating mesh nebulizers, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
