<p><p>The Activated Alumina market is primarily divided into two types: powdered form and sphered form. Powdered form activated alumina is widely used in applications such as water treatment and air purification due to its high surface area and adsorption properties. In contrast, sphered form activated alumina is engineered for specific uses like catalytic processes and gas separation, offering improved flow characteristics and packing density. Both forms are essential for various industrial applications, contributing to their growing demand in the market.</p></p>
