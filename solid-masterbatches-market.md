<p><p>The solid masterbatches market comprises various types designed to enhance plastic products. White masterbatch is used for opacity and brightness, while black masterbatch provides UV resistance and color depth. Color masterbatch enables a wide range of hues, allowing for vibrant designs. Additive masterbatch incorporates performance enhancers like stabilizers and anti-oxidants, improving material longevity. Plastic filler masterbatch adds bulk and reduces costs by substituting some of the resin, improving efficiency in the manufacturing process. Together, they cater to diverse applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
