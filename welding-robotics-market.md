<p><p>The welding robotics market comprises various types, with arc welding and spot welding being the most prominent. Arc welding uses a welding power supply to generate an electric arc between an electrode and the workpiece, ideal for thicker materials. Spot welding, often used in automotive manufacturing, involves fusing overlapping metal sheets at specific points using electrodes. Other welding methods in this market include laser welding and TIG welding, catering to diverse applications in industries like aerospace, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
