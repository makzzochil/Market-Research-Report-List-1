<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) market comprises two primary types: Layer Stranding Structure OPGW and Central Tube Structure OPGW. Layer Stranding Structure OPGW features fiber optic cables arranged in multiple layers around a central metal core, enhancing flexibility and strength. In contrast, Central Tube Structure OPGW encapsulates optical fibers within a central tube, providing robust protection and facilitating easier installation and maintenance. Both types are essential in modern telecommunications and power distribution networks.</p></p>
