<p><p>The Mobile Roughness Measuring Instrument market comprises two primary types: Stylus Type and Scanning Type. </p><p>Stylus Type instruments employ a fine probe that traverses the surface, measuring roughness by detecting vertical deviations. They offer high precision and are ideal for laboratory settings. </p><p>Scanning Type instruments utilize non-contact methods, like laser or optical scanning, to assess surface roughness over larger areas quickly. This type is suited for field measurements and various industrial applications, providing efficiency without compromising accuracy.</p></p>
