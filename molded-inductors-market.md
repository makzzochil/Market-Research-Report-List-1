<p><ul><li>Invariant Inductor</li><li>Variable Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molded inductors market is segmented into two main types: invariant inductors and variable inductors. Invariant inductors maintain a constant inductance value, making them ideal for applications requiring stable performance, such as power supplies and audio equipment. Variable inductors, on the other hand, allow for adjustable inductance, providing flexibility in tuning circuits for applications like radio frequency and signal processing. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, enhancing circuit performance and efficiency.</p></p>
