<p><strong>The Intelligent Pigging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Flux Leakage</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent pigging market encompasses three primary types: Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL), Ultrasonic, and Caliper technologies. MFL uses magnetic fields to detect corrosion and structural defects in pipelines, providing detailed information on wall thickness. Ultrasonic pigging employs sound waves to assess material integrity, identifying flaws and measuring thickness. Caliper pigs are designed to map the internal geometry of pipelines, detecting deformations. Together, these technologies enhance pipeline safety and maintenance, enabling efficient monitoring and early detection of potential issues.</p></p>
