<p><strong>The ACL Reconstruction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tissue Fixation</li><li>Bone Fixation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ACL reconstruction market is segmented into two primary types: tissue fixation and bone fixation. Tissue fixation involves devices that secure the graft within the tissues of the knee, ensuring stability and integration. Common methods include interference screws and staples. Bone fixation, on the other hand, pertains to techniques that anchor the graft to the bone, using materials like screws or anchors to promote healing. Both methods are crucial for restoring knee functionality and improving patient outcomes after ACL injuries.</p></p>
