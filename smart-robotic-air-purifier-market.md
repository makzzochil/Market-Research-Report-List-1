<p><p>The Smart Robotic Air Purifier market features several technology types. HEPA filters capture fine particles, ensuring high-efficiency filtration. Active Carbon utilizes adsorption to eliminate odors and volatile organic compounds. Electrostatic Precipitators charge particles, forcing them to adhere to collector plates. Ion generators release charged ions to attract pollutants, while Ozone Generators produce ozone to neutralize contaminants. Additionally, "Others" encompass emerging technologies or hybrid systems that combine multiple methods for enhanced air purification, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
