<p><p>The cable duct sealing system market includes two primary types: inflatable and rubber pressing. The inflatable type utilizes air-filled bags to create a tight seal around cables, offering flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, the rubber pressing type involves compressing rubber materials to secure ducts, ensuring reliable protection against environmental factors and contaminants. Both systems are designed to enhance the integrity and longevity of cable installations, catering to diverse industry requirements and providing effective sealing solutions.</p></p>
