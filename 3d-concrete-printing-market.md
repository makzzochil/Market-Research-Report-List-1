<p><strong>The 3D Concrete Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion-based</li><li>Powder-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D concrete printing market is primarily divided into two types: extrusion-based and powder-based technologies. Extrusion-based printing uses a robotic arm or nozzle to layer a thick concrete paste, creating structures through continuous material deposition. In contrast, powder-based printing involves layering dry concrete powder and selectively binding it using a liquid binder, which allows for intricate designs and complex geometries. Both methods have unique applications, influencing construction efficiency, material usage, and architectural possibilities in the construction industry.</p></p>
