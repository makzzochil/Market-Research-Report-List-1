<p><strong>The Screw-type Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-injected</li><li>Oil-free (including Dry type and Water jet type)</li><li>Liquid Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw-type compressor market is segmented into three main types. Oil-injected compressors utilize oil for lubrication and cooling, enhancing efficiency but potentially introducing oil into the compressed air. Oil-free compressors are designed for applications requiring clean air; they include dry type, which uses a direct compression design with no lubrication, and water jet type, which uses water for cooling and compression, ensuring no oil contamination. Liquid injection compressors involve injecting a liquid, often refrigerant or water, to enhance performance and cooling efficiency.</p></p>
