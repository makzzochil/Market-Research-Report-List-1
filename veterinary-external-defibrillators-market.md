<p><p>The Veterinary External Defibrillators market consists of two main types: automatic and semiautomated defibrillators. Automatic defibrillators deliver shocks without user intervention, making them suitable for emergency situations where rapid response is critical. Semiautomated defibrillators require some operator input, guiding users through the process while providing feedback on device operation. Both types are designed to restore normal heart rhythm in animals experiencing cardiac emergencies, enhancing veterinary care and improving outcomes in critical situations.</p></p>
