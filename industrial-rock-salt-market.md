<p><p>The Industrial Rock Salt market encompasses various extraction methods. Conventional mining involves extracting salt from underground deposits using traditional mining techniques, ensuring high purity. Solar evaporation utilizes sunlight to evaporate seawater in large ponds, yielding natural salt but with potential impurities. Vacuum pan evaporation is a more controlled process that heats brine under a vacuum, allowing for the production of high-purity salt crystals. Each method caters to different industry needs, influencing quality, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
