<p><p>The stainless steel ingots market is categorized into hot strapping and cold strapping types. Hot strapping involves processing ingots at elevated temperatures, enhancing malleability and ease of shaping, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, cold strapping entails processing at room temperature, resulting in increased strength and hardness, ideal for precision components. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, with hot strapping focusing on versatility and cold strapping emphasizing durability and structural integrity in finished products.</p></p>
