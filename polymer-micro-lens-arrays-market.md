<p><p>The Polymer Micro Lens Arrays market comprises two main types: Aspherical Micro-Lens Arrays and Spherical Micro-Lens Arrays. Aspherical Micro-Lens Arrays feature a non-uniform curvature, allowing for improved light focusing and reduced optical aberrations, making them suitable for applications like imaging and illumination. Conversely, Spherical Micro-Lens Arrays have a uniform curvature, providing simpler manufacturing processes and consistent performance, often used in optical sensors and fiber optics. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
