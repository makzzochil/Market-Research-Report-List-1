<p><p>The bariatric toilet market includes two main types: siphon jet and blowout jet. Siphon jet toilets utilize a siphoning action to efficiently flush waste, providing a quieter and more powerful performance, ideal for heavier users. In contrast, blowout jet toilets employ pressurized jets that create a strong, rapid flush, minimizing clogs and ensuring thorough cleanout. Both types are designed to meet the needs of larger individuals, offering enhanced support and stability while promoting hygiene and comfort.</p></p>
