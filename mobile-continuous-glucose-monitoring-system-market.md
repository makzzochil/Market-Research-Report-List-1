<p><p>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) System market is divided into two primary types: invasive and non-invasive. Invasive CGMs involve placing a sensor under the skin to track glucose levels in real-time, typically providing accurate data for diabetes management. In contrast, non-invasive CGMs, including exfoliation techniques, aim to measure glucose levels through the skin, eliminating the need for needles. Both approaches offer unique benefits, catering to different patient preferences and enhancing overall diabetes management strategies.</p></p>
