<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) masterbatches market is categorized into three main types: LDPE, HDPE, and LLDPE. LDPE masterbatches are known for their flexibility and transparency, making them ideal for films and packaging. HDPE masterbatches offer high strength and durability, often used in containers and industrial applications. LLDPE masterbatches provide excellent elasticity and puncture resistance, suitable for stretch films and flexible packaging. Each type serves specific applications, contributing to a diverse and growing market focused on enhancing performance and processing efficiency.</p></p>
