<p><p>The CO2 capture plant market includes two primary types: physical and chemical methods. Physical methods utilize adsorption or absorption processes leveraging physical changes to capture CO2, often involving solvents or porous materials. In contrast, chemical methods rely on chemical reactions, typically using alkaline solutions or amines, to form stable compounds with CO2. Both methods are critical in reducing greenhouse gas emissions, with applications across industries such as energy, manufacturing, and waste management, driving innovation and investment in carbon capture technologies.</p></p>
