<p><p>The hemoperfusion machines market is primarily divided into single-pump and dual-pump systems. Single-pump hemoperfusion machines utilize one pump to circulate blood through the adsorbent cartridge, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, dual-pump machines employ two pumps for more efficient blood flow and enhanced control over treatment parameters, allowing for greater customization and improved patient outcomes. Both types are crucial in therapeutic interventions for various conditions, significantly impacting patient care in renal and toxicological applications.</p></p>
