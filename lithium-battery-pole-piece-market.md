<p><p>The lithium battery pole piece market is divided into two main segments: cathode pole pieces and anode pole pieces. Cathode pole pieces are critical for the positive electrode in lithium batteries, typically made from materials like lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate, influencing energy density and overall performance. Anode pole pieces, on the other hand, serve as the negative electrode, usually composed of graphite or silicon-based materials, affecting charge capacity and efficiency. Both segments are essential for enhancing battery functionality and longevity.</p></p>
