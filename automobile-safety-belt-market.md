<p><strong>The Automobile Safety Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELR Three Point Belt</li><li>ALR Three Point Belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile safety belt market includes two primary types: the Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) three-point belt and the Automatic Locking Retractor (ALR) three-point belt. The ELR belt allows for unrestricted movement while locking the belt during sudden stops, ensuring maximum safety. In contrast, the ALR belt secures the occupant by automatically locking when pulled out, providing a firm fit for child safety seats. Both types enhance passenger protection, catering to different safety needs within vehicle designs.</p></p>
