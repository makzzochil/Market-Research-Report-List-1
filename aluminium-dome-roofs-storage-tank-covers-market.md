<p><p>Aluminium dome roofs for storage tanks come in two primary types: internal floating roof systems and external floating roof systems. Internal floating roofs are designed to rest on the liquid surface inside the tank, minimizing emissions and evaporation loss while preventing contamination. External floating roofs, on the other hand, float on the tank's surface externally, providing similar benefits but requiring more robust structural support. Both systems enhance safety, optimize storage capacity, and ensure compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
