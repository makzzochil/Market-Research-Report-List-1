<p><p>Digital dental radiography systems comprise various types designed to enhance diagnostic capabilities in dentistry. Ordinary radiography systems include standard intraoral devices that capture detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, crucial for cavity diagnosis and treatment planning. Panoramic radiography systems create wide-angle views of the entire jaw and teeth, helping in the assessment of complex dental and orthodontic issues. Other market segments may include specialized systems like cone beam computed tomography, which provides 3D imaging for intricate treatment planning.</p></p>
